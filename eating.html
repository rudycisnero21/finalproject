Why eating is important for self care

Energy and Metabolism:

Food provides the body with energy in the form of calories. Calories from food are converted into energy that fuels bodily functions, physical activities, and metabolic processes.

Maintains Weight and Body Composition:

A balanced diet helps maintain a healthy weight, supporting optimal body composition and reducing the risk of obesity-related diseases.

Aids in Growth and Development:

Proper nutrition is crucial for growth in children and adolescents and supports overall development throughout life.

Adopting a balanced and nutritious diet that includes a variety of foods from different food groups is essential for reaping the benefits of eating. Choosing whole foods, incorporating fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates can contribute to better overall health and vitality.
